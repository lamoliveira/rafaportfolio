About Me

Hello! I'm Rafa Bento, an aspiring animation artist passionate about bringing stories to life through visual art and motion. My journey in animation began with a fascination for how single frames can create compelling narratives and evoke powerful emotions.

Inspired by the dynamic storytelling of comic books and graphic novels, I blend traditional animation techniques with modern digital tools to create unique visual experiences. My work explores the intersection of character development, movement, and narrative flow.

I'm currently seeking opportunities to expand my skills and knowledge in animation at leading colleges in the United States, where I can collaborate with fellow artists and push the boundaries of visual storytelling.

My approach to animation is rooted in understanding character psychology and environmental storytelling. I believe that every frame should serve the narrative while maintaining aesthetic appeal and technical excellence.

When I'm not animating, you can find me sketching in coffee shops, studying frame-by-frame breakdowns of my favorite animated films, or exploring new digital art techniques and software.

I'm excited to bring my unique perspective, dedication, and creativity to a college program that values innovation and artistic growth.
